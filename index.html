Paper Shredder

Alice Comi

The Oxford Handbook of Media,
Technology, and Organization Studies

This chapter shows how the paper shre-
dder mediates aspects of confidentiality,
transparency, and secrecy in organizations.
Following Susan Star Leigh’s ‘tricks of the
trade’, it conceptualizes the paper shredder
as material infrastructure and retraces its
organizing force and effects. This brings
into view virtuous as well as vicious prac-
tices in which the paper shredder becomes
implicated—ranging from the protection of
sensitive data, to the covering of unethical
practices. The chapter concludes with
suggestions to reconceptualize material
infrastructures in organizations, and unveil-
related aspects of confidentiality, trans-
parency, and secrecy.

Keywords: confidentiality, transparency,
secrecy, material infrastructures,objects,
scandals
